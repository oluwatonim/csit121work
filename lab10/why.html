Why is Audiobook important?

Audiobook is important because it helps disabled student and struggling readers get more vocabulary and learn more words through listening.It introduce student to book above their reading level. It introduce new genres that students might not consider.It also provide a read aloud model. It can be slowed down and fasten for the student who needs it.

Audiobook is also important causse it make disabled and non reader equaly the same and they understand a little better. It makes them fit in with other and be able to comprehend the same way others do.

It helps enhance literature skills and have good comprehension.It makes you feel like you in a cloud trying to imagine what is been read out loud.
