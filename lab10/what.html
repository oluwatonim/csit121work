What is Audiobook?

Audiobook is a recording of text that always readers to listen to a Book. It makes reading a book fun and convinent. Its available on commnon apps like Apple Books and the most common Audible.com. Audiobook makes it easy to listen and do other things like washing dishes, laundry while listening to your Favorite book.

Audibook can be created by any writer. Some common books we read also have a audiobok available. The Duration of audiobook ranges but mostly 1 hour and above. Its fun for people who enjoy reading in a audio way.

Audiobook can help younger kids that can't read yet use the form of listening to understand some stuff.
